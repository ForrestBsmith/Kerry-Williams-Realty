Financing typically starts by getting pre-approved through a mortgage lender or bank. We can connect you with trusted loan officers who will guide you through the process and help determine your buying power.

  • Get pre-approved before you start shopping.
  • Work with a licensed real estate agent who understands your market.
  • Always schedule a professional home inspection.
  • Understand your budget and stick to it.
  • Review HOA or deed restrictions when applicable.

To list your property, fill out our online form or contact us directly. An agent will consult on pricing and marketing, prepare your listing, coordinate showings, and help negotiate offers through closing.

You can reach us anytime at info@44realtygroup.com or call our office during business hours. We're happy to help with buying, selling, or general inquiries.
